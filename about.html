About Micro-Courses Platform

The Micro-Courses Platform is designed to make digital literacy easy and accessible for everyone. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by offering short, interactive lessons on essential online skills.

🎯 Our Mission

We aim to empower under-resourced communities, beginners, and lifelong learners by teaching them how to safely and confidently use digital tools in everyday life.

📌 What You’ll Learn

  • How to create and manage an email account
  • Using Google Docs for work and study
  • Staying safe while making online payments
  • Responsible social media use
  • Online communication and etiquette
  • Basic cybersecurity practices

🤝 Who Can Benefit?

This platform is built for students, seniors, job seekers, small business owners, and anyone new to the digital world. No prior experience is required.

🚀 Why Micro-Courses?

Each lesson is designed to be short, practical, and beginner-friendly. You can complete a course in 10–20 minutes and immediately apply your new skills.
